IRC Channels

The Bitcoin Nova community utilizes a lot of IRC channels that each serve different purposes. Some to work, and some just to hang out. You'll find the more popular ones below.

 

    #bitcoinnova

    This channel is used to discuss all things Bitcoin Nova related.

      #bitcoinnova-community

      This channel is for the Bitcoin Nova community to congretate and discuss ideas.

        #bitcoinnova-dev

        The many contributors and developers come here to discuss dev things.

          #bitcoinnova-markets

          We use this channel to talk about the price of Bitcoin Nova and other coins.

            #bitcoinnova-offtopic

            Chatting with other Bitcoin Nova users about things not related to Bitcoin Nova.

              #bitcoinnova-pools

              This is the place for mining questions and discussion.

                #bitcoinnova-translations

                Localizing Bitcoin Nova into other languages.

                  #bitcoinnova-hardware

                  Building hardware wallets to keep your Bitcoin Nova safe.

                    #bitcoinnova-marketing

                    We use this channel to talk about how to advertise the currency.
